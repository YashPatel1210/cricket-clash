# 06 - Glossary

## Draft

The complete player selection process.

---

## Draft Round

One Playing XI presentation.

---

## Playing XI

The eleven players presented during one draft.

Not the user's team.

---

## Team

The final eleven-player squad created by the user.

---

## Team Selection

A player together with:

- Batting Position
- Captain Flag
- Wicket Keeper Flag

---

## Match

Simulation between two completed teams.

---

## Match Environment

Shared conditions:

- Stadium
- Pitch
- Weather
- Toss
